My Autistic World

Welcome to my Autistic Life

It has been quite a journey to get to this point, after a lifetime of misunderstanding why I am the way I am.

At the age of 36, my family doctor came to the realization that I am Autistic and referred me to the Adult Autism Diagnostic Clinic at the Glenrose Rehabilitation Hospital in Edmonton, AB (I am still waiting to be assessed).

Resources I Have Found Helpful in my Journey

  • "Women and Girls with Autism Spectrum Disorder: Understanding Life Experiences from Early Childhood to Old Age" written by Sarah Hendrickx
  • "Spectrum Women: Walking to the Beat of Autism" edited by Barb Cook and Dr. Michelle Garnett
